About

Summary of our whole story

  • 2009-2011

    Staring phase

    We have started a offline store for selling jeans. Gradually we had opened 5 stored in a city Ahmedabad in 2010 and 2 new stores in surat.

  • 2011-2014

    We exponentially enlarged our business and started selling t-shirts and shirts in 2013.

  • 2015-2018

    Transition to Clothing Brand

    In 2015, we started our brand with name pure-jeans to make jeans only but gradually increase in product range to all kidswear and menwear and female fashion. our focus is mainly on mens and kids fashion.

  • 2018-till now

    Phase Two Expansion

    In 2018, we started out business online. we had our own website and we are trying to be available on android and ios platform.
